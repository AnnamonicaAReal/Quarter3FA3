The Three Diamonds of Homeworld

Long ago, in the distant reaches of space, the mighty empire of Homeworld was ruled by three great Diamonds: Blue Diamond, Yellow Diamond, and White Diamond.

Each Diamond held supreme power, commanding vast armies of Gems, shaping worlds, and maintaining order across the galaxy.

However, beneath their rule, whispers of rebellion began to stir. Some Gems questioned their purpose, wondering if there was more to existence than serving their Diamonds.

Blue Diamond, the ruler of sorrow and compassion, mourned for the Gems lost in conquest, her heart weighed by grief.

Yellow Diamond, the embodiment of strength and discipline, sought to crush opposition and maintain the order of Homeworld at any cost.

White Diamond, the most powerful of them all, demanded absolute perfection, believing that unity could only exist through her control.

As unrest grew, the Diamonds convened in their grand palace, their forms towering over their subjects.

"Homeworld must remain strong," Yellow Diamond declared. "We cannot allow weakness to fester."

"But our Gems are suffering," Blue Diamond lamented. "Should we not listen to their cries?"

White Diamond, her radiant glow illuminating the chamber, simply watched in silence before speaking. "Order must be preserved. We will decide the fate of our empire together."
